Final Inspection of Grid Tie Solar Power Plant

A week long final Inspection of the Grid connect solar PV power plant in Kirtipur was carried out by the system integrator Coretec Inc. along with the system installer Suryodaya Urja and other partner organisations from June 26 to July 5 2013. The power generation station which was completed a year ago was found to be running smoothly. On the final day of inspection one of the solar modules was noticed damaged, which was replaced the following day. Apart from annual inspection Suryodaya Urja is carrying out a monthly inspection for smooth operation of the solar power plant until August 2013 after which all inspection activities also come under the beneficiary KUKL.
